# 5-Stufen-Entscheidungs-Empfehlung

See `deepwork-env-setup/references/decision-levels.md` for the full template.

Kategorie B (Recherche-Tiefe) is the primary category used in deepwork-01:
- Stufe 1: Überblick (1–2 Agents)
- Stufe 2: Basis (3–5 Agents)
- Stufe 3 ⭐: Standard (6–10 Agents) — default for most projects
- Stufe 4: Tiefgehend (10–20 Agents)
- Stufe 5: Exhaustiv (20+ Agents)
